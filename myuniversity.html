My University

BMCC

Borough of Manhattan Community College (BMCC) is a prominent two-year college located in the heart of Manhattan, New York City. As part of the City University of New York (CUNY) system, BMCC offers a wide range of associate degree programs and serves as a vital educational institution in the city. The college is known for its diverse student body, exceptional faculty, and a commitment to providing accessible, affordable, and high-quality education to its students. BMCC is also well-regarded for its strong academic programs, including offerings in areas like business, health sciences, and the liberal arts. With a central location in one of the world's most dynamic cities, BMCC provides students with ample opportunities to connect with industry professionals and gain valuable real-world experience.
