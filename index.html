Hello, I'm Kalelo Dukuray, a computer science major currently enrolled at BMCC. I'm excited to share that I'll be graduating this semester, and my aspirations are set on pursuing further studies at the prestigious Georgia Institute of Technology. My interests have always revolved around the world of mathematics, computer science, and the fascinating realm of cryptography. Looking ahead, my ultimate goal is to contribute to the development of open-source software that safeguards individuals' privacy and fundamental human rights. I firmly believe that technology holds the key to our future, and I advocate for everyone to acquire at least a basic understanding of programming.
